What is ATOM?

Matter is made up of small particles called ATOMS.ATOM is smallest building block of matter.

Properties of Atom

  • ATOM is made up by three particles:
    1. Electron
    2. Proton
    3. Neutron
  • Nucleus is made up of proton and neutron
  • Electron is revolving around a Nucleus
